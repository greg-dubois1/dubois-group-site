Du Bois Group is an independent healthcare parent group established in 2013, bringing together clinical practice, professional education, and advisory work under a single, coherent structure.

The Group’s origins lie in the delivery of UK-accredited clinical training to UK organisations operating overseas, and its work has evolved alongside the founder’s progression through frontline emergency care, Advanced Clinical Practice, education design, quality improvement, and strategic service development.

Today, Du Bois Group provides the umbrella for three distinct but connected strands of activity:

PRACTICE — Du Bois Clinical Services
Advanced clinical and specialist paramedic services across primary, urgent, and emergency care settings.

EDUCATE — AMEGA Training
Professional education resources, clinical training programmes, mentoring and coaching, and ACP portfolio development.

CONSULT — Axiom Consulting
Advisory work supporting quality improvement, workforce capability, and digital transformation.

The Du Bois Group exists to provide clarity, continuity, and governance for work that has evolved over more than a decade, rather than to represent a newly created venture.
